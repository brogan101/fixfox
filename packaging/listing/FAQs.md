# Fix Fox Listing FAQ

## Q1: Does Fix Fox require internet?
No. Core diagnostics, fixes, and export packaging run locally on the machine.

## Q2: Does Fix Fox upload my data?
No automatic upload is performed. You choose what to export and where to share it.

## Q3: What is a Home Share Pack?
A light support pack designed for home users with share-safe masking enabled.

## Q4: What is a Ticket Pack?
An MSP/IT-focused support pack that includes deeper evidence bundles by default.

## Q5: Can I run Fix Fox without admin rights?
Yes. Safe read-only and low-risk flows run without admin; admin-only actions are clearly gated.

## Q6: Are files deleted automatically?
No. Cleanup flows are preview-first and require explicit confirmation before changes.

## Q7: Can I cancel long tasks?
Yes. Long-running tasks execute in workers and can be cancelled from ToolRunner.

## Q8: Can I validate exports before sharing?
Yes. Manifest and hash validation runs during export with share-safe checks.

## Q9: What does share-safe masking do?
It masks common identifiers such as host, user-path, and SSID patterns in supported outputs.

## Q10: Does Fix Fox support rollback guidance?
Yes. Fix details include risk, expected changes, and rollback guidance where applicable.

## Q11: Can I reopen past sessions?
Yes. History supports reopening prior sessions and re-exporting without rerunning tools.

## Q12: Is Fix Fox suitable for MSP workflows?
Yes. Ticket Pack + evidence bundles and runbooks are designed for helpdesk/MSP triage.
